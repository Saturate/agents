# Sources

Rules are based on these references. Fetch for deeper analysis or to check for updated patterns.

## Primary rule sources

- [realrossmanngroup/no_ai_slop_writing_rules](https://github.com/realrossmanngroup/no_ai_slop_writing_rules) - Anti-slop rules with worked examples and banned word reference
- [The Field Guide to AI Slop](https://www.ignorance.ai/p/the-field-guide-to-ai-slop) - Structural and vocabulary detection signals, inflated symbolism frequency multipliers
- [The AI Ick](https://stackoverflow.blog/2025/11/05/the-ai-ick) - Tone and voice signals, "all product no struggle" pattern
- [Wikipedia: Signs of AI Writing](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Wikipedia:Signs_of_AI_writing) - Banned word lists, academic tells, hedging thresholds, structural patterns
- [Most Common ChatGPT Words to Avoid](https://walterwrites.ai/most-common-chatgpt-words-to-avoid/) - Vocabulary-level tells

## Research

- isgpt.org corpus analysis (2025) - Inflated symbolism frequency multipliers (468x, 317x, etc.)
- ACL hedging study (2024, 12,000 technical documents) - AI models hedge 4-7x more than human writers
